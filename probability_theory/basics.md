# Set 
- empty set: $\emptyset$
- A is subset of  B: $A \subseteq B$
- union of A and B: $A \bigcup B$
- intersection of A and B: $A \cap B$
- complement of A: $A^c$
- De Morgan's laws
$$
\left( A \cup B\right)^{c} = A^c \cap B^c 
$$
$$
\left( A \cap B\right)^{c} = A^c \cup B^c 
$$
