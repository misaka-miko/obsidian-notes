> Bayes's rule deals with how we update our belief

# General definition
Given event $A$ and $B$, we have
$$
P(A|B) = \frac{P(A)P(B|A)}{P(B)}
$$
**notation explanation**:
- $P(A|B)$: posterior probability
- $P(A)$: prior probability
- $P(B|A)$: likelihood
